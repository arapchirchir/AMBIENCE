Why Choose Ambience?

Personalized attention

Every child being attended to at the facility will follow a personalized treatment plan that is created following a thorough assessment of their past struggles, current needs and future goals

Compassionate and professional

From your initial phone call or visit, through the admission process, you can expect to be attended to in close collaboration with experienced professionals dedicated to providing superior care with dignity and respect

Clinical Excellence

Ambience hospital is committed to providing clinically exceptional services that incorporate both time-tested techniques and research supported methodologies

Since inception, we handled and continue to take care of children's health needs throughout the greater North Rift and Western Kenya regions.

We always strive to create an everlasting bond with our patients by continually following up on their healthcare needs.

Ambience receives patients from local communities that comprise schools, Churches, individuals and groups. Our warm inviting medical center takes pride in keeping Children healthy in addition to treating the sick.

We are here when you need us, and working even harder to keep your Children healthy to ensure they do the things they enjoy most without sickness induced hindrance.

Ambience Hospital was established in the year 2020 as the first full range pediatric hospital in the North Rift region.

The facility is located in Eldoret town along Nandi road, and prides itself for always being at hand to offer professional care and treatment to Children. The hospital is equipped with some of the best facilities and we are open from 8:00AM-8:00P.M. We combine taking care of patients with a commitment to excellent medical care. Our team of pediatricians and care givers continually attend workshops and trainings in order to keep them abreast with new developments and advancements.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive high quality medical services that is accessible and affordable to the community, to promote health, to educate the healthcare professionals and to participate in appropriate clinical research.

Our Vision

To be an innovative, leading regional health facility dedicated to advancing the health and transforming the lives of the people we serve through excellent clinical quality, accessible patient-centered caring service and unmatched physician and employee commitment.

Services & Facilities

DENTAL

We offer dental care for children. The dental unit is well equipped to handle all dental care needs and challenges children may encounter. The department is manned by fully qualified and certified pediatric dentists, oral and maxillofacial surgeons

LABORATORY

The laboratory provides a wide range of services to our esteemed clients. This is our innovation hub where we test and calibrate for safety, reliability and overall integrity to inspire efficiency.

PHARMACY

Our pharmacy is well stocked with a variety and relevant drugs that are adequate to cover any emerging situation. The facility has evolved and become valuable to clients because of creation of a neighborhood pharmacy feel atmosphere

IMMUNIZATION

Being a Children's hospital, we also offer immunization services ranging from E.P.I recommended vaccines as well as other vaccines recommended by World health Organization (WHO). We will help you and family stay updated on recommended vaccines either on walk-in basis or scheduled appointments.

WELL BABY CLINICS

This service comprises monitoring and evaluating children’s health. It includes weight checks and milestone checks and overall growth and development follow up

EYE CLINIC

The unit is manned by a team of qualified and certified ophthalmologists who are on call throughout.

IMAGING AND RADIOLOGY

Our facility is equipped to handle radiology and imaging needs. We have in place ultrasound machine E.E.G, E.C.G and echo equipment. We outsource any equipment that we don't have. This unit is served by both consultants and technicians.

DAY CARE SERVICES

We have well equipped observation rooms where sick children are stabilized before they are either discharged or referred to a hospital for admission

PEDIATRIC SURGICAL SERVICES

We have in place an efficient pediatric surgical service provided by our competent pediatric surgeons.

PLAYGROUND

We have in place a playground and other facilities to keep our children happy and busy as they go through their healing process
